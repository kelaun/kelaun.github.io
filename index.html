About

About Me

I'm Kevin Andriessens, a photographer dedicated to finding beauty in authentic moments.
With a keen eye for detail and emotion, I specialize in creating compelling visual stories across various photography disciplines. My approach combines technical precision with artistic creativity, ensuring each image captures not just appearances, but feelings and memories.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new destinations with my fiancée Laura, constantly seeking fresh perspectives and inspiration. My passion for travel enriches my photographic vision, while my love for my two dogs reminds me daily of the joy in capturing spontaneous moments.

What sets my work apart is my commitment to creating a comfortable, collaborative experience for every client. Whether I'm photographing your beloved pet, crafting your professional portrait, or documenting your special event, I bring the same attention to detail and dedication to excellence.
