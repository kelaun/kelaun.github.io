About Kelaun Photography

Kevin Andriessens is an amateur photographer considering becoming professional in the near future.

Kevin's work covers wedding, beauty, landscape, and portrait photography for both businesses and individuals. Having started his career in information technology, he is also comfortable and actively enjoys building websites which he can have available for his customers. Kevin's ethos is that 'variety is the spice of life' and that 'visual philosophies for creating effective images are universal'.

Kevin regularly travels around the globe, as a photographer.

Outside of work, Kevin is happily engaged to Laura. They enjoy traveling to unusual and challenging destinations accross the world. When not taking pictures, Kevin spends much of his time with his two dogs.
